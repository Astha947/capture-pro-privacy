Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026

✓ ZERO DATA COLLECTION

We collect absolutely nothing.

Capture Pro does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data, usage data, or any other information about you or your device. Everything stays on your machine.

📋 Overview

Capture Pro is a Chrome extension that allows users to record their screen and take screenshots. This Privacy Policy explains how Capture Pro handles user data.

The short version: we don't collect anything. No accounts. No servers. No tracking. No analytics.

🔒 Data We Do NOT Collect

Capture Pro does not collect any of the following:

  • Personally identifiable information (name, email, address)
  • Screen recordings or screenshots
  • Browsing history or web activity
  • Location data
  • Usage statistics or analytics
  • Crash reports
  • Any form of telemetry

💾 Local Storage Only

Capture Pro uses Chrome's local storage solely to remember your preferences (such as whether microphone, webcam, or system audio is toggled on or off). This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted anywhere.

🎥 Screen Recordings & Screenshots

All recordings and screenshots captured using Capture Pro are saved directly to your device's Downloads folder. They are never uploaded to any server, cloud service, or third party. We have no access to your recordings or screenshots at any time.

🌐 Permissions Used

Capture Pro requests the following Chrome permissions, used solely for the stated purposes:

  • activeTab — To capture the current tab as a screenshot
  • tabs — To identify the active tab for capturing
  • storage — To save your toggle preferences locally
  • scripting — To inject the area selection overlay on web pages
  • downloads — To save recordings and screenshots to your Downloads folder
  • tabCapture — To capture screen content for recording
  • host permissions — To inject the area selection content script on web pages

👶 Children's Privacy

Capture Pro does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since we collect no data at all, there is no risk of children's data being stored or shared.

📝 Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date. Since we collect no data, we do not anticipate meaningful changes.
